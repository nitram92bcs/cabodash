SHOCK MOUNT

REPAIR

Shock mounts are the rubber absorbers that hold the back of the chair to the seat. Over time, the shock mounts are susceptible to failure and will need repair. We use custom jigs depending on the year of your chair so the positioning is perfect and manufacture our own shock mounts here in the USA made exactly to spec as the originals. Unlike most repair shops we will never drill holes through your eames chair. We utilize the proper black two-part adhesive made specifically for bonding rubber to wood.
